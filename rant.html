Latest Rant: Trigger Happy Havoc

So recently I've gotten quite obsessed with the game Dangan Ronpa or Danganronpa, which ever is correct as I've seen the spacing change constantantly.

The first time I had heard of the game was when I watched a MLP thing that had apparently taken a lot cues from the game. As that fandom continues to seem to do me, I was baffeled as realativly famous reviewers from their community were brutally murdered by some demon thing for having opionions differing from hers.The spectacle got me curious but not enough so to actually see the show. As a side note the deaths in that far exceed the gruesomeness in the actual show... because when ponies get involved the first you think of brutal brutal murder.

I first really caught intrest in it because of the abridged series. Which I found odd as that has never happened to me before. Neverbefore had I stopped watching an abridged series so not to spoil me of what happened in the show. Not for Sword Art Online, Full Metal Alchemist, Code Geass, Attack on Titan, not a one grabbed me like this. I'm not even really sure why either. Nothing in that first episode really sticks out in my head as to why I Had been so interested in it. Maybe it was just because it was a mystery and I wanted to figure it out myself.

The shows I've watched recently have had a lot trouble with staying good all the way through (R.I.P Samurai Flamenco) so this was a very nice change of pace.
